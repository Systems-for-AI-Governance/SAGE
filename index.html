SAIGRC

System for AI Governance, Risk, and Compliance

Background

  • AI is transforming healthcare, with nearly 1,000 FDA-cleared medical devices incorporating AI, and adoption is accelerating across healthcare IT and device vendors.
  • However, incorporating AI into healthcare poses several challenges including issues affecting patient safety, privacy, security, bias, transparency, explainability, reproducibility, equity and interoperability.
  • The technology is complex and is moving fast - outputs are stochastic and not deterministic.
  • New regulations are arising, many of which have high penalties for non-compliance and will be enforced starting 2025.
  • Liability risk arising from the use of these AI models is yet another barrier preventing healthcare providers from deploying them.

Our Solution

The System for AI Governance, Risk, and Compliance (SAIGRC) is the world's first open-source Governance, Risk Management and Compliance platform designed specifically for healthcare AI. It addresses the critical challenges of integrating AI into a high-risk domain like healthcare, where issues such as patient safety, privacy, bias, transparency, and regulatory compliance are paramount.

SAIGRC provides a structured collaboration tool kit built on clinically informed practices aligned with:

  • NIST AI RMF framework
  • FDA's SaMD Guidelines
  • ONC's HTI-1 Rule
  • HHS-OIG Information Blocking Rule ($1 million penalty for non-compliance)
  • ISO 42001 Controls

Features of SAIGRC

Governance

  • Prioritizes AI projects based on organizational goals
  • Surfaces risks early and throughout the project lifecycle
  • Ensures compliance with evolving legal and regulatory requirements

Risk Management

  • Tracks and helps mitigate risks such as data drift, system changes, and liability exposure
  • Monitors performance metrics over time

Compliance

  • Facilitates audit readiness for ISO 42001
  • Maintains a paper trail as the AI project progresses
  • Demonstrate proper discharge of fiduciary duties in case of lawsuits

Collaboration

  • Empowers all stakeholders - clinical, administrative and executive - to participate in the development and integration of the AI project

Benefits of SAIGRC

Operational Oversight

  • Real-time updates on incidents
  • Centralized view of AI projects and their states

Risk and Performance Monitoring

  • Ensures that risks affecting the project and the organization are surfaced early, surfaced often and surfaced throughout the lifecycle of the project
  • Continuous tracking of ROI and strategic alignment

Compliance and Accountability

  • Enables adherence to emerging regulations
  • Strengthens contract negotiations and legal defensibility

Collaboration and Customization

  • Provides a platform for multi-stakeholder participation
  • Open-source design avoids vendor lock-in and allows customization
