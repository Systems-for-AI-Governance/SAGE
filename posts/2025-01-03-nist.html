How SAIGRC Aligns with the NIST AI RMF

SAIGRC aligns with the NIST AI RMF functions: Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage, ensuring robust AI governance, risk management, and compliance for healthcare organizations.

Govern

  • Governance Prioritization: Aligns AI projects with organizational goals, focusing on impactful initiatives.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensures adherence to frameworks like ISO 42001, SaMD, and HTI-1.
  • Stakeholder Collaboration: Engages cross-functional teams in AI governance efforts.
  • Audit Trails: Maintains transparent and well-documented compliance records.

Map

  • Downstream Impact Analysis: Evaluates technical, social, and ethical implications of AI deployment.
  • Technical Suitability Assessment: Ensures approaches align with operational goals.
  • Regulatory Gap Identification: Tracks compliance with standards like FDA guidelines and ONC rules.

Measure

  • Performance and Bias Tracking: Monitors system reliability and fairness.
  • Risk Assessment Tools: Tracks vulnerabilities like data drift using repeatable methods.
  • Lifecycle Metrics: Simplifies evaluation across design, deployment, and decommissioning phases.

Manage

  • Proactive Risk Mitigation: Monitors risks across the AI lifecycle.
  • Incident Reporting: Provides real-time updates on issues and mitigation steps.
  • Lifecycle Management: Supports updates, adaptations, or decommissioning seamlessly.
  • Model Auditing: Conducts ongoing audits to maintain effectiveness.

By embedding these principles, SAIGRC ensures iterative alignment with the NIST AI RMF, helping healthcare organizations maintain compliance, safety, and operational excellence.
